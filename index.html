Step 1

Past lane: yesterday decides the family

If the sentence is in the past, the form agrees with the subject’s gender or number.

  • был for masculine singular: он, брат, учитель
  • была for feminine singular: она, мама, сестра
  • было for neuter singular: письмо
  • были for plural: они, дети, друзья, мы

Step 2

Future lane: tomorrow decides the person

If the sentence points forward, choose the future form from the subject: я / ты / он-она / мы / вы / они.

  • буду / будешь / будет / будем / будете / будут track person and number
  • With an infinitive after it, keep the same future form: будут играть, будете смотреть
  • вы takes будете whether plural or polite singular

The learning loop

Designed for fast lock-in

  1. Notice the signal first: past or future.
  2. Choose from tight contrasts before you see mixed drills.
  3. Get immediate feedback with the reason, not just the answer.
  4. See your weak forms again after a short delay.
  5. Finish with mixed retrieval so the pattern holds without hints.
Important: я in the past depends on the speaker’s gender: я был or я была.

The chart

Possessor chooses the family. Noun chooses the ending.

Use the row for the owner, then use the noun column. Some rows never change: его, её, их stay the same across all four columns.

How to read it

Two fast decisions

  • я / ты / мы / вы take families that change by column
  • он / оно share его
  • она stays её
  • они stays их
  • Masculine, feminine, neuter, and plural nouns each pull a different ending

Training loop

Built for quick retrieval

  1. Warm up the changing families first.
  2. Mix the full chart once the endings stop wobbling.
  3. Track each chart cell separately, not just the overall score.
  4. Bring back slow or missed cells sooner.
  5. Finish with mixed nouns so the chart works outside the table.
Important: mastery is accuracy plus speed. A cell is only steady when you can reach it fast.
